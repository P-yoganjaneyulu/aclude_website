Our Process

Our Audit Process

A systematic approach to comprehensive accessibility assessment

01

Initial Consultation

We begin with a detailed discussion to understand your specific needs, space type, and compliance requirements. This helps us customize our audit approach for maximum relevance.

Duration: 30-60 minutes
02

Comprehensive Site Assessment

Our certified accessibility experts conduct thorough on-site evaluations covering all 20+ accessibility parameters, including physical barriers, design elements, and user experience factors.

Duration: 2-5 hours (varies by size)
03

Standards Evaluation

Each finding is evaluated against national and international accessibility standards including Indian guidelines, WCAG, and ADA requirements to ensure comprehensive compliance.

Duration: 1-2 days
04

Detailed Reporting

We prepare comprehensive reports with detailed findings, photographic evidence, priority rankings, and practical implementation recommendations tailored to your budget and timeline.

Duration: 3-7 days
05

Implementation Support

We provide ongoing guidance and support during implementation, including design specifications, contractor recommendations, and quality assurance to ensure successful execution.

Duration: Project-dependent
