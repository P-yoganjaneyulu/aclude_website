A systematic approach to successful accessibility project delivery

01

Planning & Scoping

We begin with comprehensive project planning including scope definition, timeline creation, resource allocation, and risk assessment to establish a solid foundation for success.

Duration: 1-2 weeks
Requirements Analysis Timeline Development Resource Planning Risk Mitigation
02

Coordination & Setup

Duration: 1 week

Duration: Project-dependent
Daily Monitoring Quality Checks Progress Tracking Issue Resolution
