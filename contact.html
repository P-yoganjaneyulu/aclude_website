Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does an accessibility audit take?

Most accessibility audits are completed within 2-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of the space. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with existing buildings?

Yes! We specialize in retrofitting existing buildings and structures to meet accessibility standards. Our team can work within budget constraints and minimal disruption requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Tamil Nadu, with offices in Thoothukudi and Chennai. We also work on projects across South India and can arrange travel for special projects.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on project scope and requirements. We offer free initial consultations and will provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.
