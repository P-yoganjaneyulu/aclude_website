Our Process

Our Design Process

A systematic approach to creating accessible and inclusive spaces

Site Assessment & Analysis

Thorough evaluation of existing conditions, user requirements, and accessibility challenges to inform the design strategy.

Concept Design & Strategy

Development of initial design concepts and accessibility strategy that balances aesthetics, functionality, and universal design principles.

Detailed Design Development

Creation of comprehensive technical drawings, specifications, and documentation ready for implementation and construction.

Implementation Support

Ongoing consultation and support during the construction phase to ensure design integrity and accessibility compliance.
