Creating Inclusive Environments for All

Building Accessibility Excellence Across India

With over 7 years of dedicated experience, Aclude Foundation has emerged as India's premier accessibility consulting firm. We've audited 479+ places and made 264+ spaces fully accessible, serving 127+ organizations across government, corporate, and community sectors.

Our mission is simple yet profound: ensuring equal access for every person, everywhere. From comprehensive audits to complete construction and training programs, we provide end-to-end accessibility solutions.
